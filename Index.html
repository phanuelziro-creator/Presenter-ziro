Presenter Ziro (Phanuel Ziro)

Presenter Ziro is a Kenyan media personality, presenter, educator and digital content creator based in Kilifi County. His mission is to inspire positive change through storytelling, education, documentaries, podcasts and impactful community conversations.

He believes media should not only entertain but also educate, inspire and transform society.
